## Introduction

`MapboxTemperature` renders GeoJSON point features as a native Mapbox heatmap based on a temperature property. `weightProperty` specifies the weight field, and `weightRange` linearly maps its values to heatmap weights. `radius`, `intensity`, `opacity`, and `colorStops` control the visual output.

## Usage

Visualize the heatmap distribution of city temperature samples — adjust `radius` to see how the spread changes:

::component-example
---
name: TemperatureExample
prettier: true
options:
  - name: radius
    label: radius
    default: 40
    items:
      - 20
      - 40
      - 60
---
::
