## Introduction

`MapboxLights` calls `setLights` to set the map's 3D lighting list — `ambient` (ambient light), `directional` (directional light), and `flat` (flat light) — applied to 3D buildings and terrain. Requires a style with 3D content (such as `standard`) and a tilted camera angle.
